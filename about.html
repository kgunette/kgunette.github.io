About

What connects all my projects is the same impulse: find a complex problem with no obvious solution, and build one. I thrive on teams of smart people with different perspectives and low ego, working on something genuinely difficult. Cross-pollination across sectors has built a deeper toolkit that strengthens every new problem I take on.

I work best in rooms where people are genuinely trying to figure something out. I bring optimism, a sense of humor, and a bias toward big ideas that become concrete things in the world. I approach the work with empathy and accountability, and I think hard problems deserve people willing to sit with complexity rather than reach for the easy answer.

People tend to come to me for two things: strategic advice (mapping the steps from where you are to where you want to be) and thought partnership on problems without obvious answers. I'm consistently good at inventing new things people actually use, learning new domains fast (race cars, retail innovation, auto hardware, benefits policy), and building work processes from scratch where they don't exist yet.
