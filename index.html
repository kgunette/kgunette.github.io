Karen Schoellkopf

I build new capabilities for organizations tackling complex problems—from startups navigating regulation to established institutions driving innovation.

I'm a product and strategy leader who thrives in ambiguous, high-stakes environments. I founded and scaled Leap Fund—one of the nation's first platforms addressing benefits cliffs, from concept to national reach, raising $2M+ in competitive nonprofit funding and testifying before Congress on policy implications. Before that, I spent 10+ years leading product at early-stage startups and driving digital transformation for Fortune 500 companies. I specialize in building solutions where there's no playbook: navigating regulatory constraints, managing multi-stakeholder dynamics, and turning vision into execution in resource-constrained environments.

I build 0-1 products, programs, and strategies in complex environments. This includes:

  • Product strategy & development: Designing and launching platforms, tools, and services from concept through scale
  • Strategic initiatives: Identifying new business opportunities, running experiments, and building business cases for new ventures
  • Systems & community design: Creating solutions that work across tech, policy, operations, and human behavior
  • Organizational change: Translating vision into executable programs while navigating stakeholder complexity

I've worked across startups and Fortune 500s, in regulated and emerging industries, but what's consistent is the ability to build in ambiguity, constraint, and complexity.
