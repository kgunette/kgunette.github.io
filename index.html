Karen Schoellkopf

I build new capabilities for organizations tackling challenging problems, from startups navigating regulation to established institutions driving innovation.

I'm a product and strategy leader with nearly 20 years of experience thriving in ambiguous, complex environments. I spent the last 7 years founding and scaling Leap Fund, one of the nation's first platforms addressing benefits cliffs, from concept to national reach, raising $2M+ in competitive nonprofit funding and testifying before Congress on policy implications. Before Leap Fund, I spent 10+ years leading product at early-stage startups and driving digital transformation for Fortune 500 companies, building on a decade of managing complex creative production for world-renowned artists. I specialize in building solutions where there's no playbook: from regulated industries to emerging technology sectors, managing multi-stakeholder dynamics and turning vision into execution in resource-constrained environments.


I build 0-1 products, programs, and strategies in complex environments. This includes:

  • Product strategy & development: Designing and launching platforms, tools, and services from concept through scale
  • Strategic initiatives: Identifying new business opportunities, running experiments, and building business cases for new ventures
  • Systems & community design: Creating solutions that work across tech, policy, operations, and human behavior
  • Leadership & execution: Translating vision into executable programs while building teams and navigating stakeholder complexity
